# Adoption Guide

![The V.E.N.K.A.T Framework for Agentic AI](assets/venkat-framework-for-agentic-ai.svg)

## Step 1: Assess Data Trust

Evaluate data quality, lineage, observability, ownership, and contracts.

## Step 2: Identify Critical Business Events

Map real-time signals that should trigger AI reasoning or workflow automation.

## Step 3: Add Spatial Context

Identify assets, customers, infrastructure, vehicles, facilities, and geographic dependencies.

## Step 4: Build Knowledge Relationships

Create entity relationships across customers, orders, products, assets, locations, policies, and events.

## Step 5: Orchestrate AI Agents

Define agent roles, tool access, workflows, escalation paths, and human-in-the-loop checkpoints.

## Step 6: Govern AI Actions

Apply policy controls, audit logs, explainability, security, financial thresholds, and compliance rules.
