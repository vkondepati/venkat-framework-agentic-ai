# Manufacturing Use Case: Warehouse and Plant Digital Twin

The V.E.N.K.A.T Framework can support manufacturing operations by combining verified operational data, real-time machine and inventory events, native spatial intelligence inside warehouses or plants, knowledge graphs for asset-product-process relationships, AI orchestration for workflow execution, and governance for safe automation.

Example outcomes:
- Space optimization
- Inventory movement recommendations
- Predictive maintenance
- Worker safety alerts
- Automated work order generation
