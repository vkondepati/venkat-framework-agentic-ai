# Logistics Use Case: Autonomous AI Dispatch Agent

## Scenario

A major highway closure blocks 50 delivery trucks carrying customer shipments.

## Traditional Architecture

A traditional data platform may show the delay on a dashboard, requiring human operators to investigate, reroute, check inventory, and communicate with customers.

## V.E.N.K.A.T Framework Response

### V — Verified Data
Validates traffic feeds before acting.

### E — Event-Driven Architecture
Triggers a real-time highway closure event.

### N — Native Spatial Intelligence
Analyzes truck locations, alternate routes, road constraints, warehouse proximity, and risk zones.

### K — Knowledge Graphs
Understands shipment priority, SLA exposure, inventory relationships, and customer impact.

### A — AI Orchestration
Coordinates routing, inventory, customer communications, and operations agents.

### T — Trust & Governance
Checks authorization, policy limits, auditability, and compliance before action.

## Outcome

Within minutes, trucks are rerouted, critical shipments are reallocated, customers are notified, and all actions are logged.
