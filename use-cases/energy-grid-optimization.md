# Energy Grid Optimization Use Case

## Scenario

A utility needs to manage feeder stress, distributed energy resources, electric vehicle charging, outage risks, and customer demand while maintaining reliability and regulatory compliance.

## V.E.N.K.A.T Response

### V - Verified Data

Smart meter data, feeder telemetry, outage records, asset registries, DER data, customer programs, and weather feeds are validated and governed.

### E - Event-Driven Architecture

Meter events, feeder alerts, DER changes, weather warnings, and EV charging patterns trigger operational assessment.

### N - Native Spatial Intelligence

Spatial intelligence models feeder geography, substations, service territories, outage zones, customer clusters, EV density, and infrastructure proximity.

### K - Knowledge Graphs

The graph connects customers, meters, transformers, feeders, substations, DER assets, tariffs, programs, weather zones, and operational constraints.

### A - AI Orchestration

Agents recommend load balancing, smart charging actions, DER coordination, field crew prioritization, and customer communications.

### T - Trust & Governance

Policies control operational thresholds, regulatory constraints, customer consent, safety requirements, and escalation rules.

## Example Outcomes

- Feeder stress detection.
- EV load disaggregation.
- Smart charging recommendations.
- DER capacity analysis.
- Governed load balancing.
- Auditable operational decisions.
