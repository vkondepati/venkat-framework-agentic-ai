Reference Architecture

Trusted autonomous enterprise systems need more than a model.

They need verified data, live events, spatial context, connected knowledge, orchestrated action, and governance designed as one operating architecture.

Abstract

A practical architecture model for governed enterprise autonomy

The V.E.N.K.A.T Framework defines a six-layer enterprise architecture for Agentic AI systems that can observe trusted signals, understand operational context, reason over relationships, coordinate action, and improve through governed feedback.

Version 1.0 positions Agentic AI as an architecture discipline spanning data trust, event-driven platforms, spatial intelligence, knowledge graphs, AI orchestration, and accountable governance.
